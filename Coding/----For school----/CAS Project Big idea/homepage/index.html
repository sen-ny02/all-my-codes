Browsing the web without caution can expose you to various dangers, especially as a student. You might encounter harmful content, cyberbullying, or even fall victim to online scams and identity theft. It's crucial to be mindful of the websites you visit, avoid sharing personal information, and be wary of suspicious links and downloads. Remember, staying safe online is just as important as staying safe in the real world.
